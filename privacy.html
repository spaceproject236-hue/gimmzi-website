Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  |  Gimmzi LLC

Gimmzi Smart Rewards ("Gimmzi", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user of the Gimmzi app and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Gimmzi:

  • Account information: Name, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account.
  • Location data: General location to show nearby participating merchants. We do not continuously track your location.
  • Transaction data: Records of qualifying purchases and deal redemptions used to calculate your Gimmzi Balance.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support and app improvement.
  • Usage data: How you interact with the app, including features used and offers viewed, to improve your experience.
  • Community and travel partner data: If you are a resident at a participating apartment community or book travel through a Gimmzi travel partner, we receive confirmation of that activity to issue applicable badge grants.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Gimmzi account
  • Calculate and display your Gimmzi Balance
  • Process deal redemptions and loyalty program activity
  • Send notifications about your balance, membership tier, and available offers
  • Improve the Gimmzi platform and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and abuse of the rewards program

3. Gimmzi Balance and Rewards Data

Your Gimmzi Balance is a promotional reward currency and does not represent monetary value. All earning activity, redemption history, and balance data is stored securely and associated with your account. This data is used solely to operate the rewards program. See our Terms of Service for full details on the nature of Gimmzi Balance.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With participating merchants: Only the information necessary to verify a qualifying transaction or redemption (e.g., that a scan occurred at their location).
  • With service providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate the platform (e.g., payment processors, cloud hosting providers) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • For legal compliance: When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of Gimmzi, its users, or the public.
  • In a business transfer: If Gimmzi is acquired or merges with another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you in advance.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and transaction data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for analytics purposes.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Withdraw consent for location data collection in your device settings

To request account deletion or exercise any of these rights, visit our Account Deletion Request page or contact us at privacy@gimmzi.com.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest, secure authentication, and regular security reviews to protect your information. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable precautions to protect your data.

8. Children's Privacy

Gimmzi is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or by email. Your continued use of Gimmzi after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@gimmzi.com
  • Mail: Gimmzi LLC, Wilmington, NC
